Colors

Color Theory & Theming Strategy

The accent color system uses temperature-based theming to optimize visual comfort and hierarchy across light and dark modes, grounded in color psychology and perceptual contrast principles.

By inverting color temperature relative to each background, both themes maintain optimal legibility, emotional resonance, and visual balance—ensuring consistent perceptual impact while reducing eye fatigue across different lighting conditions.

Dark Mode (Warm Jewelry Gold): The rich gold (#B8860B) delivers vibrant contrast against cool dark backgrounds. Its warm, luxurious quality evokes craftsmanship and value, perfect for an art marketplace in low-light environments.

Light Mode (Cool Emerald Green): The fresh emerald (#059669) provides strong chromatic contrast against warm white backgrounds. Green's association with growth, creativity, and authenticity aligns perfectly with handmade art and photography.
